And what is pollution?

Pollution is the introduction of harmful materials to the evironment

Effects of pollution

As a matter of fact pollution has adverse effects such as;

  1. Leads to global warming
  2. Causes infertility of the land

Solutions to pollution

To curb this issue of pollution,the following might be done;

  • Recycle and refuse waste materials
  • Reduce use of plastic bags
  • Reduction of forest fires and smoking
  • Use of public transport
